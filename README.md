# Econometrics Project: Impact of Macroeconomic Landscape on Academic Pursuit 	
**Topics:** Econometrics Analysis, Multivariate OLS, Quadratic Regression, Multiollinearity

**Tools:** R, 'readxl', 'car'

## Overview
- Developed multivariate OLS models in R to analyze a 38-year longitudinal dataset, incorporating quadratric transformations to capture nonlinear economic effects. 
- Mitigated multicollinearity and omitted variable bias through auxiliary regressions and Variance Inflation Factor (VIF) analysis. 
- Executed Joint Significance (F-tests) to compare the explanatory power of macroeconomic driers, identifying statistically significant differences in enrollment elasticity.
